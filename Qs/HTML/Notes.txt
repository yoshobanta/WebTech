Html Tag has been classified into 2 types:
1. Semantic Tag
2. Non Semantic Tag

1. Semantic Tag :These are the tags which will clearly define its meaning and the functionality just by reading them. These tag come into wide use from Html5.
Some of the Semantic tag are front, em, strong.

2. Non Semantic Tag : Does not define its meaning or functionality of their content.
Some of the non semantic tag are bold, i div etc.
